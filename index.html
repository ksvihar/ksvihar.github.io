WHO I AM

Well yes! I am a Graphic Designer. I see the world a bit different, in a colorful wrap with much geometry around. Getting inspired and translating that into designs is what I enjoy the most. I like to think out of the box, work hard at finding simplicity in design, even if it expresses something complicated. I usually like it flat and minimal. If there are two things that make me who I am, they are designing and football. I’m a Gooner and going to the Emirates stadium, right up there on top is on my bucket list. An hour-a-day of football is a day well spent. Well, this is what my right part of the brain does.

Fortunately, the left works just fine too! It absorbed whatever I learnt in the five good years I spent in BITS, Pilani. I wish I could be more technical and explain what I did in engineering, but I don’t really remember much.

There are two things I believe with all my heart – It doesn’t matter what shoes you’re in, it matters what you do when you’re in them, and that today, is the first day of the rest of my life, so? Would I live life to the fullest?! Oh yes I would.
